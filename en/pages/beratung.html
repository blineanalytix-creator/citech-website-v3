EU AI Act

The Four Risk Classes

Violations can result in fines of up to EUR 35 million or 7% of annual global turnover.

Minimal Risk

Voluntary codes of conduct

  • Spam filters
  • AI in video games
  • Recommendation systems
  • Automatic spell check

Limited Risk

Transparency obligations

  • Chatbots and conversational AI
  • Emotion recognition
  • Deepfake generation
  • AI-generated content

High Risk

Strict requirements & CE marking

  • AI in human resources
  • Biometric identification
  • Critical infrastructure
  • Credit scoring

Unacceptable Risk

Prohibited applications

  • Social scoring by public authorities
  • Subliminal manipulation
  • Exploitation of vulnerable groups
  • Real-time remote biometric identification

Our Mission

Compliance Consulting

We guide organizations toward legally sound and ethically responsible use of AI — with a clear focus on pragmatic implementation rather than excessive bureaucracy.

Regulatory requirements should not stifle AI innovation, but place it on a secure foundation.

Our 5 Principles

1

Listen Before Judging

We understand your business processes before imposing compliance requirements.

2

Iterative, Not Big Bang

Step-by-step implementation with quick wins, not multi-year mega-projects.

3

Empower, Not Patronize

We train your teams so you can increasingly operate independently.

4

Document With Measure

As much as necessary, as little as possible — compliance documentation should help, not paralyze.

5

Long-Term Partnership

Compliance is not a one-time project. We stay by your side as regulations or your AI landscape evolve.

What We Do Not Do

Spread Fear

We do not rely on panic rhetoric — we work with objective risk assessment.

Overcomplicate

No bloated compliance frameworks that create more administrative overhead than value.

Substitute Legal Counsel

We are consultants, not attorneys. For sensitive legal questions, we refer you to specialized law firms.

Remain Technology-Agnostic

We understand the AI systems we advise on — we reject superficial compliance checks without technical understanding.
