1. Privacy at a Glance

The following information provides a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is any data with which you could be personally identified.

Our AI-powered chatbot CIRA processes your inputs solely to answer your questions. No personal data is stored. 4. Data Collection on This Website

Cookies

Our website uses so-called cookies. Cookies are small text files that do not cause any damage to your device. They are stored on your device either temporarily for the duration of a session (session cookies) or permanently (persistent cookies).

Contact Form

If you send us inquiries via the contact form, your details from the inquiry form, including the contact data you provide there, will be stored by us for the purpose of processing the inquiry and in case of follow-up questions. We do not share this data without your consent.

Legal Basis: Processing is carried out on the basis of Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual measures) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in processing your inquiry).

Inquiry by Email or Phone

If you contact us by email or phone, your inquiry including all resulting personal data (name, inquiry) will be stored and processed by us for the purpose of handling your request.

Legal Basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual measures) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in efficient communication).

6. Newsletter (AI Radar)

We offer the free "AI Radar" newsletter, which provides weekly updates on current developments in the field of Artificial Intelligence.

Registration and Double Opt-In

Registration is done via a form on our website. After entering your email address, you will receive a confirmation email (double opt-in). Only after clicking the confirmation link will you be added to the mailing list.

Data Collected

  • Email address (required)
  • Name (optional)
  • IP address and user agent at the time of consent (proof of GDPR-compliant consent)
  • Time of registration and confirmation

Email Service Provider

The newsletter is sent via Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), Brevo SAS, 106 Boulevard Haussmann, 75008 Paris, France. Brevo is a GDPR-compliant service provider with servers in the EU. Brevo Privacy Policy: https://www.brevo.com/de/legal/privacypolicy/

AI-Generated Content

The newsletter content is partially created with the support of AI systems (Claude AI, Anthropic) and subsequently editorially reviewed and approved. Further details can be found in our AI Transparency Statement.

Legal Basis

Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent). You may revoke your consent at any time. Each newsletter issue contains an unsubscribe link, which allows you to unsubscribe with a single click.

Unsubscribing

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time via the unsubscribe link in each email or by sending a message to m.batur@citech-ai.de. After unsubscribing, your data will be immediately deleted from the mailing list.
