Quality Seal

AZAV-certified Training Provider

CITECH is certified as a training provider under AZAV (Accreditation and Licensing Regulation for Employment Promotion) by TÜV NORD. This means: Our training programs meet the strict quality requirements of the German Federal Employment Agency (Bundesagentur für Arbeit). AZAV certification is the German government's quality standard for publicly funded vocational training.

Certification Details

  • Certification body: TÜV NORD CERT GmbH
  • Certificate number: 44 727 251914
  • Valid until: 28.12.2030
  • Scope: Vocational training measures pursuant to Sections 81, 82 of the German Social Code III (SGB III)

What AZAV Means for You

  • Verified quality of training content and delivery
  • Prerequisite for funding via education voucher (Bildungsgutschein)
  • Regular external audits by TÜV NORD
  • Binding quality standards in didactics and organization

Qualification Opportunities Act

The Qualification Offensive

Since April 2024, funding options for vocational training in digitalization and AI have been significantly expanded in Germany. Employees and companies can benefit from government support under certain conditions.

Training Costs

Course fees can be partially or fully covered by the Federal Employment Agency, depending on your individual situation.

Wage Subsidies

Employers may receive a subsidy for wages during the training period under certain conditions.

During Working Hours

Training can take place during working hours. Our courses are designed as full-time on-site programs.

Honest Note on Funding Amounts

The actual funding amount depends on several individual factors:

  • Your current employment status (employed, job-seeking, on short-time work)
  • The size of your company (micro-enterprise, SME, large enterprise)
  • Your federal state and the responsible employment agency
  • Your individual prerequisites and professional situation

We do not promise blanket funding rates but provide individual and realistic advice on your personal options.

Your Path to Funding

5 Steps to Funded Training

We guide you through the entire process – from the initial consultation to the start of your training.

1

Consultation

In a free initial consultation, we analyze your situation: employment status, qualification goals and possible funding paths. Honest and without obligation.

2

Document Collection

Together we compile all required documents: qualification certificates, employer confirmations and the appropriate program application.

3

Application Submission

You submit the application to your local employment agency. We support you with the wording and provide all program-related documents.

4

Approval Review

The employment agency reviews your application. Processing typically takes 2 to 4 weeks. We are available as the training provider to support with any inquiries.

5

Training Start

After approval, you begin your AI training at the next available date. You focus entirely on learning – cost processing runs directly through the agency.
