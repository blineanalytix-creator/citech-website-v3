03 — AI in Training

AI as a Teaching Tool, Not the Teacher

In our training courses, we use AI systems exclusively as demonstration objects and practice tools. All content is delivered by certified instructors.

Systems Used

System Purpose Data Privacy
Claude / ChatGPT Text generation, Prompt Engineering, Code assistance Cloud-based, no data storage
Stable Diffusion Image generation (local) 100% local, no cloud

Cloud Services in Training: For demonstration purposes, we use cloud services (OpenAI, Anthropic). All exercise data is completely deleted after the course ends. Participants are instructed not to use sensitive data.

Training Content

Our training materials (presentations, handouts, exercises) are partially created with AI support and subsequently undergo comprehensive expert review:

  • Presentation slides: AI-assisted, fully reviewed by experts and didactically adapted
  • Exercises: Partially AI-generated, pedagogically validated and aligned with learning objectives
  • Code examples: Developed with AI assistance, all tested and documented
  • Handouts: AI-assisted creation, validated by experts and kept up to date
04 — Quality Assurance

VERIFY Process for AI Content

Every AI-generated piece of content goes through our structured quality process.

1

V — Validate

Expert review by qualified specialists. All AI outputs are checked for accuracy, relevance, and quality.

2

E — Edit

Adaptation to target audience, corporate language, and didactic requirements. Removal of errors or inaccuracies.

3

R — Review

Second review by an independent reviewer. Four-eyes principle for critical content.

4

I — Integrate

Integration into existing structures with consideration for consistency and corporate identity.

5

F — Finalize

Final approval by management. Last quality check before publication.

6

Y — Yield

Publication with clear AI labeling and continuous quality monitoring in live operation.

Principle: No AI-generated content is published without human review and approval. Human-in-the-Loop is our standard.

07 — Sustainability

Responsible AI Usage

We are aware of the environmental impact of AI systems and make conscious decisions to conserve resources.

Energy Efficiency

Preference for smaller, more efficient models where possible. Use of local systems (Ollama, Stable Diffusion) reduces data center load.

Resource Conservation

Avoidance of unnecessary AI requests. Caching of results. Deliberate use instead of automated mass production.

Education & Awareness

Training our clients in energy-efficient AI usage. Addressing sustainability aspects in all courses.

Local Alternatives

Where possible, we use local AI systems (Ollama, Local LLMs) instead of cloud services. This reduces data transfer and server load.
