Our Core Product

Offline RAG Systems

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) connects your existing documents with an AI language model — completely local, without internet, on your hardware.

What does this mean in practice?

Ask your company knowledge questions in natural language — and receive precise answers with source references. PDFs, Word documents, wikis, databases: everything searchable, everything offline.

How the RAG Pipeline Works

1

Ingest Documents

PDFs, Word files, wikis, emails, databases — your existing data sources are automatically captured and processed.

2

Structure Knowledge

Content is semantically indexed, split into chunks, and stored as vectors in a local database.

3

Ask Questions

Employees ask questions in natural language — as simple as a Google search, but on your own data.

4

Precise Answers

The system finds the relevant passages and generates a well-founded answer — always with source references and fully traceable.

5

Everything on Your Infrastructure

Not a single byte leaves your network. No cloud, no external API calls. Full data sovereignty, full control.
